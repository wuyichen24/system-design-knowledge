# Non-technical factors need to be considered for selecting a storage solution

- Proven stability. Whether the storage system has been used by other big companies in the same industry for many years (for example, more than 5 years) with positive feedback.
- The price of the targeted storage system and the budget you have.
- The size of the community of the targeted storage system.
- The richness of supporting tools, such as monitoring and investigation tools.
- Maturity of the database administrator (DBA) job market.
